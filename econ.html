Economics Principles

Section 1: Supply and Demand

Supply is the amount of a good producers are willing to sell. Demand is the amount consumers want to buy. Equilibrium occurs where they meet.

Section 2: Market Structures

Types include perfect competition, monopoly, oligopoly. Each affects pricing and output.

Section 3: Macroeconomics

Study of the economy as a whole: GDP, inflation, unemployment.

GDP = Consumption + Investment + Government Spending + Net Exports

Quiz: Economics Basics

What does GDP stand for?

  • A) Gross Domestic Product
  • B) General Demand Price
  • C) Global Development Plan

Answer: A) Gross Domestic Product
