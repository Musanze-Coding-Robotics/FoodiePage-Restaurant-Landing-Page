Biology Fundamentals

Section 1: What is Biology?

Biology is the scientific study of life and living organisms. It includes everything from microscopic cells to entire ecosystems.

Key branches: Botany (plants), Zoology (animals), Microbiology (microbes).

Section 2: Cells - The Building Blocks of Life

All living organisms are made up of cells. Cells are the basic structural and functional units of life.

Types: Prokaryotic (bacteria) and Eukaryotic (plants, animals).

Section 3: Photosynthesis and Respiration

Photosynthesis is the process by which plants convert light energy into chemical energy. Respiration is the process of releasing energy from food.

Equation for photosynthesis: 6CO₂ + 6H₂O + light → C₆H₁₂O₆ + 6O₂

Quiz: Biology Basics

  • B) Mitochondria
