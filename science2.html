Chemistry Basics

Section 1: What is Chemistry?

Chemistry is the scientific study of the properties and behavior of matter. It is a physical science that studies atoms, molecules, and compounds.

Key areas: Organic chemistry, Inorganic chemistry, Physical chemistry.

Section 2: Atoms and Elements

An atom is the smallest unit of an element that retains the properties of that element. Elements are pure substances made of only one type of atom.

The periodic table organizes elements by atomic number.

Section 3: Chemical Reactions

A chemical reaction is a process where substances (reactants) are transformed into different substances (products).

Example: 2H₂ + O₂ → 2H₂O (water formation).

Quiz: Chemistry Basics

What is the chemical formula for water?

  • A) CO₂
  • B) H₂O
  • C) NaCl

Answer: B) H₂O
